Welcome

Home & Garden Editorial Services (HGES) packages books for publishers in the home, garden and related fields. We excel at producing both design-oriented idea books as well as detailed how-to guides for do-it-yourselfers. HGES also produces articles for major national shelter and how-to magazines. Joe Provey, who has more than 20 years of experience in the field, leads the team. Joe is the former chief editor of several national publications, including The Family Handyman, Mechanix Illustrated and Practical Homeowner. He was building editor at House Beautiful magazine.

To contact us, please write to joeprovey@handgedit.com, or to Home & Garden Editorial Services 44 Hackley Street Bridgeport, CT 06605 Or, call 203-367-5221.

Services

Writing

Our published writers create clear, concise content for manuscripts, articles, newsletters, brochures, menus, direct-mail pieces, Web site content and whatever else you may need. If a writer with expertise in a certain subject is required, we’ll hire one if he or she isn’t already on staff.

Design

Our team of experienced designers and illustrators — all of them have experience working on books and at national magazines — will work with you to produce eye-popping projects, large or small.

Layout

Our designers are proficient in QuarkXPress and are committed to providing creative, pleasing printed materials. They can work from a template or create a new design, incorporating text, photos, illustrations, maps, charts, graphs and other elements.

Editing

Editing is much more than simply searching for misspelled words, and our editors, who have college degrees and years of related experience, handle the myriad tasks. Those tasks include copyediting (checking for spelling, grammar, punctuation and consistency in style), proofreading (reviewing hard copy for typographical and formatting errors), and fact checking.

Indexing

Is there anything more boring and more tedious than creating an index? Let us do the boring, tedious work for you. We create professional indexes in-house, taking great care in choosing the terms that will be listed — we don’t leave that job to a computer.

Photo Research

If your project requires photos, we’ll get them. Perhaps we already have what you need in stock. If we don’t have what you need, we go straight to the source, whether that’s a freelance photographer, a manufacturer, an organization or a stock photography agency.

Photography and Art

We have talented photographers and illustrators on staff if original photography or artwork is required. Our photographers can also handle prepress issues.
